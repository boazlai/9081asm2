**Background Fact**

## 4. Checkpoint 2 ¬∑ Site of 452 Nathan Road

_(now a modern building opposite Tin Hau Temple)_

**Walk 9 minutes north along Nathan Road**

`[IMAGE_PLACEHOLDER_9x16 ‚Äì 452 Nathan Road / tong lau location]`

**Background Fact**  
In the 1950s‚Äì70s this was a typical tong lau with mahjong parlour building. On the night of 4 March 1953 thousands gathered to watch the ‚Äúhaunted flat‚Äù.

**Urban Legends (the famous mahjong ghost cluster)**

**Modern Reinterpretation**  
Mahjong is social glue in Hong Kong. By imagining the dead still playing, the city keeps victims inside the community rather than isolated in death. The joss-paper punchline is dark humour ‚Äî classic Hong Kong coping mechanism.
